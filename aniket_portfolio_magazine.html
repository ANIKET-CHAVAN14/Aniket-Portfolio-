About Me
The story behind the person

I am a first-year Computer Science Engineering student at MIT Vishwaprayag University, driven by curiosity that stretches far beyond the limits of a single discipline — from algorithms and code to anatomy, biology, and preventive healthcare.

I have mentored NEET and JEE aspirants, guiding students through two of India's most demanding examinations. Teaching biology isn't just a skill — it's a passion that sharpens how I approach every new challenge I face.

I believe leadership is about building people, not managing them. Administrative and leadership roles have sharpened my ability to organize, communicate, and inspire those around me.

Outside academics, I'm a drummer with rhythm in my veins, a business thinker always spotting opportunities, and someone who deeply believes in the power of prevention over cure — in health, communities, and systems.

"My goal is to become a Preventive Officer — building systems that protect communities before problems arise."
